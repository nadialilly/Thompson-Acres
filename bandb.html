BED AND BREAKFAST
On The Farm

This little santucary is in the heart of Ponte Vedra. A unique Bed and Breakfast expericence awaits you at our farm house. Your family can discover the joys of the simple life by feeding the alpacas or gather eggs from the hen house.

We have a Full, farmers breakfast served family-style around 8am. This is not something you want to miss with our fresh fromthe garden vegetables and eggs. You can put in a prefence of how you would like your eggs and pancakes at check-in. Not an early riser? No problem we have an assorment of bagels, pasteries and coffee offered until 11am.

We have some fun for all ages. Try your hand at axe Throwing or archery. We have some bow and arrow for the kids and some for proffessionals.

If you would like to do some off roading we have a four wheeler that can be rented. You can take it out on our trails or let the speed be your guide down the back trail. keep an eye out for all our furry friends.

If you just looking for some outdoor fun you can make use of the trampoline, swinging spider web, bikes, or take a walk on our board walk through the marsh.

We also have gas grills and smokers for use. There is also three different fire pit areas and lots of options to just sit back and enjoy the animals and views.

The farm animals. We have pigs, fainting goats, alpacas, chickens, ducks, turkeys, dogs and a sheep. they are all friendly and ready to be visited and played with at anytime during the day. Almost all our animals grew up here as babies and have been around children of all ages.
