Our Mission

Thompson Acres is a place where middle schoolers learn in simplicity and community, discovering and exploring the environment around them. They have the opportunity to build skills for life year after year.

Activities

  • Fedding and caring for the animals
  • Cooking with vegetables picked from the garden
  • Nature walks through swamps and trails
  • Homework time
  • Free play - on Trampolines
  • Archery and Axe throwing

Hours

Monday-Friday 2pm-5:30

*Holiday will follow the St. Johns County school calendar

We do NOT provide care on teacher inservce days

Pricing

  • 3-DAYS: $150 a week
  • 5-DAYS: $225 a week
